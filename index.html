About Us

Domicile Dominini Football Academy is a youth-centered academy dedicated to nurturing talent, building discipline, and inspiring greatness on and off the pitch. Based in Kenya, we focus on technical development, fitness, mental strength, and academic excellence.

Our Programs

  • Under-10 Boot Camp: Early technical foundation and fun.
  • Under-15 Elite Squad: Intermediate skills and tactical training.
  • Senior Team (U-18): Competitive level preparation and exposure.
  • Weekend Clinics: Open training for walk-ins and community youth.
  • Scholarship Program: For top talents from needy backgrounds.
